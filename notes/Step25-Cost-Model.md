# Step25: Cost Model

## What is Pay-as-you-go?
Pay-as-you-go means you only pay for the resources you use.

---
## Examples
- EC2: charged based on running time
- Lambda: charged per execution
- S3: charged based on storage usage

---
## Why It Matters
Traditional infrastructure requires upfront investment.
Cloud allows flexible cost management based on actual demand.

---
## Relationship to Scaling
As demand increases, both resource usage and cost increase.
As demand decreases, cost decreases.
Cost can increase rapidly with scale if not properly managed.

---
## Relationship to SaaS
Cloud and SaaS both use flexible pricing models.
- Cloud: infrastructure-level pricing
- SaaS: application-level pricing

---
## Key Concept
Cost = usage-based

---
## Summary
Cloud pricing aligns cost with demand, enabling efficient and scalable system design.
