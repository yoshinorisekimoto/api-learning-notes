# Step23: Scaling

## What is Scaling?
Scaling is the ability to adjust system capacity based on demand.

---
## Why Scaling is Needed
Systems must handle varying levels of traffic.
Without scaling, systems can become slow or unavailable under high load.
Scaling helps maintain availability by handling increased load without system failure.

---
## Auto Scaling (EC2)
Auto Scaling automatically increases or decreases the number of EC2 instances based on traffic.

---
## Lambda Scaling
Lambda automatically scales based on incoming requests.
No manual configuration is required.

---
## Key Concept
Scaling = adjusting capacity to maintain performance and availability

---
## Summary
Scaling ensures systems remain stable and responsive by dynamically adjusting resources based on demand.
